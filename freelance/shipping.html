Shipping

Shipping

All in-stock items are dispatched within 2 business days of receiving payment and shipping times are estimated at between 2-5 business days depending on your location. We deliver products Australia wide using CourierPlease and Mainfreight door-to-door online tracking courier service at a competitive rate. Shipping costs are influenced by the size and weight of the product and your location, exact shipping costs are calculated in the shopping cart and will be added to the order’s total before checkout.


All “Pre-Sale” products generally takes 3-4 weeks for the arrival, unless otherwise specified.


We make every effort to make our prices as low as possible. Therefore, some of the large “Customer Order Request” products such as bonnets, doors and trunks will generally takes 4-12 weeks depending on the arrival time of our next container, as these products are not economically feasible to be air-freighted unless extra charges can be agreed upon. Please contact us prior to ordering if u wish to have the product air-freighted.


Street Element Australia does ship orders internationally using DHL Express. However, we currently do not accept international orders through our website, please contact us via our email at info@streetelement.com.au for international orders.


Shipping Damage

Street Element Australia carefully packs and checks all products prior to shipping. However, damage may still occur occasionally. Buyer’s must inspect the packaging and the products carefully upon receiving them, before accepting the shipment and notate any damages and irregularities, such as crushed, torn, punctured, or broken parts on both the packaging and the product, on the freight bill and receipt at the driver’s presence. Buyer must retain all cartons, packing materials, and damaged products for the carrier’s damage inspector to inspect. Do not refuse shipment or return it without approval, as the buyer’s right to make a damage claim may be denied. A signed receipt without any notation indemnified the carrier as well as Street Element Australia from any further damage claims. Discovery of damages after a clear delivery becomes the responsibility of the customer.


All damages must be notified to carrier and Street Element Australia within 24 hours. In cases of damage with proper notation on receipt, the carrier will determine whether to pay for repair cost or replacement value of the damaged goods after damage inspection. Street Element Australia will provide any information necessary to assist with the claim. Street Element Australia is not responsible for items lost or damaged.


Returns

All sales are final and cannot be returned unless it is covered by our Fitment Guarantee Policy or the product is defective. Sales and discounted orders cannot be returned. All products returned must be in the original shipping carton in the same condition as it was originally delivered or shipped. Defective items may be exchanged for the same item only. Street Element Australia will organise and pay all return freight charges.


Shipping Errors

If the buyer receives an incorrect item due to our error, the buyer must inform us within 3 days of receipt of the shipment. We will then organise the pick-up of the incorrect item(s) and ship the correct item(s) at no additional charges.
